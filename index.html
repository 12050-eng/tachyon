World's First
Smart Biometric Mouse

Experience real-time health monitoring directly at your fingertips. No extra wearables required. Designed for professionals and gamers to perform at their peak without compromising their long-term well-being.

The Problem

In today's fast-paced digital world, office workers, professionals, and gamers spend hours anchored to their desks. The result? Unseen stress accumulation, rising blood pressure, and long-term physical injuries like office syndrome.

Current health wearables are intrusive, battery-draining, and easily forgotten. We need a solution that integrates seamlessly into the tools we already touch every single day.

Core Features

🖱️

Pro Mouse Functions

Designed for power users. Experience flawlessly responsive DPI adjustment, programmable macro hotkeys, and laser-precise pointer capabilities for presentation and productivity superiority.

✋

Vertical Ergonomics

A masterfully sculpted vertical design that enforces a natural handshake position. Significantly reduces wrist strain, forearm pronation, and the long-term risk of carpal tunnel syndrome.

🧩

Modular Architecture

Hardware that adapts to you. Includes assembling features such as a detachable fingertip ring component, transforming your desktop mouse into a powerful remote presentation controller.

Biometric & AI Capabilities

🩸

Medical-Grade Sensors

Equipped with built-in SpO2 and Heart Rate monitors. Subtly reads your biometrics through normal contact with the mouse surface, turning your palm into a diagnostic hub.

🧠

AI Prediction Engine

Utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to process raw data and instantly predict Non-Invasive Blood Pressure (NIBP), Respiratory Rate (RR), and subtle emotional stress states.

⚠️

Real-Time Health Alerts

Never push yourself to the breaking point again. The integrated health alarm system instantly alerts you visually to abnormal stress or fatigue levels, prompting necessary breaks.

Seamless Ecosystem

Your health data shouldn't be fragmented. The Tachyon Companion App syncs instantly, tracking your physiological data across days, weeks, and months in a centralized hub.

  • Comprehensive dashboard tracking your daily HR and SpO2 trends.
  • AI-driven personalized health recommendations and posture alerts.
  • Exportable health analytics to share with your medical professionals.
  • Gaming overlay mode for streamers tracking adrenaline in real-time.
